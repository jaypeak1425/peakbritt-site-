The Gap

When what you’ve built outgrows ordinary planning.

Most firms are built for volume — a policy, a rollover, a quick illustration. But a closely held business, a concentrated estate, a key executive you cannot afford to lose: these don’t fit an off-the-shelf answer. Left unaddressed, they quietly become the forced sale, the tax the family never planned for, the succession that was never funded. Sophisticated wealth deserves planning built to the same standard as the wealth itself.

Who We Are

Built on a simple conviction.

The families and business owners who build real wealth deserve planning built to the same standard — personal, sophisticated, and honest. We grew tired of watching advanced strategies get referred out, watered down, or sold by people who didn’t fully understand them.

So we built the opposite of a volume shop. We go deep, we stay small, and we work personally with every client and every advisor we partner with. When you work with PeakBritt, you work with the people who design your plan — not a brand three layers removed from the advice.
